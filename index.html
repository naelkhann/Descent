How To Play:

1.Your ship will follow your mouse pointer. Hover over game screen and move mouse to move your ship.

2.To fire, hold down your left-click on your mouse. To stop firing, let go of your mouse click.

3.Eliminate as many enemy ships to obtain a higher score without getting destroyed.

Good luck, pilot. Click 'Start' to play.
